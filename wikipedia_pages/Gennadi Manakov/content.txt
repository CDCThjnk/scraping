Gennady Mikhailovich Manakov(Russian:Геннадий Михайлович Манаков; 1 June 1950 – 26 September 2019)[3]was a Soviet and Russian cosmonaut who commanded two Soyuz flights to theMirspace station.
He was born in Yefimovka,Chkalov Oblast, Russian SFSR, on 1 June 1950.[4]He was selected on 2 September 1985 and flew as Commander onSoyuz TM-10andSoyuz TM-16, before retiring on 20 December 1996.[5]He was married with two children. He died on 26 September 2019 according to a statement from his friend, CosmonautMaksim Surayev.[6]
