Major GeneralAnatoly Vasilyevich Filipchenko[a](26 February 1928 – 7 August 2022) was a Sovietcosmonautof Ukrainian descent. He flew on theSoyuz 7andSoyuz 16missions.
He was born inDavydovka,Voronezh Governorate,RSFSR.[1]After leaving the space programme in 1982, Filipchenko became the deputy director of theOKBinKharkiv.[1]He died on 7 August 2022, at the age of 94.[2]He was buried in theFederal Military Memorial Cemeteryon 11 August 2022.[3]
