Vyacheslav Dmitriyevich Zudov(Russian:Вячесла́в Дми́триевич Зу́дов, 8 January 1942 – 12 June 2024) was aSovietcosmonaut.

Biography
Zudov was selected as a cosmonaut on 23 October 1965, flew as commander onSoyuz 23on 14–16 October 1976, and retired on 14 May 1987.[1]He was married and had two children. Zudov died on 12 June 2024, at the age of 82.[2]He was buried in theFederal Military Memorial Cemeteryon 14 June.[3]

Awards
 - Hero of the Soviet Union
 - Pilot-Cosmonaut of the USSR
 - Jubilee Medal "Twenty Years of Victory in the Great Patriotic War 1941-1945"
 - Jubilee Medal "In Commemoration of the 100th Anniversary since the Birth of Vladimir Il'ich Lenin"
 - Jubilee Medal "50 Years of the Armed Forces of the USSR"
 - Medal "For Merit in Space Exploration"(Russian Federation)
